Do a full "handmade + warm" redesign pass on the Skifte Collective landing page and auth page. The goal is to make it feel like a beloved community zine crossed with a premium lifestyle brand — NOT a tech startup, NOT AI-generated, NOT corporate. Think: hand-lettered signage, farmer's market warmth, community bulletin board energy, but elevated and beautiful.

### 2. Organic shapes instead of perfect rectangles
- The sunshine yellow hero panel (right side): change from rounded-3xl to an SVG blob shape
  Use this blob clip-path or a CSS clip-path like: `clip-path: polygon(5% 0%, 95% 3%, 100% 95%, 3% 100%)`
  Add some scattered small dots or a hand-drawn texture inside it

- Section backgrounds: instead of hard color blocks, use slightly irregular top/bottom edges with SVG waves that look more organic and less perfect

### 3. Typography personality
- The hero h1: wrap each line in its own div, make line 1 slightly smaller, line 2 BIGGEST (the "things" line), line 3 medium — like a hand-lettered sign
- Add a handwritten-style subheading font feel using font-style: italic on key phrases
- Section headings: add a small illustrated element before or after — a leaf emoji 🌿, a small star ✦, something warm

### 4. Texture and warmth

- The "How It Works" cards: give them a slight cream/off-white background with a subtle hand-drawn border feel — use `border: 2px solid` with a slightly imperfect look via box-shadow: `2px 3px 0px #C96A3A40`

### 5. Community circles section

- Add a small 🌿 or 🏡 or 🌳 before each circle name (already there but make them bigger)
- The section heading "Find your people." — add a hand-drawn underline SVG beneath it in white/cream

### 6. Footer warmth
- Add a small hand-drawn heart or leaf decoration

## AUTH PAGE
- The card should feel like a beautiful notecard, not a form
- Add a warm welcome message above the tabs: "Welcome home." in Fraunces italic, terracotta
- The background should have the dot pattern but also maybe a subtle warm gradient from cream to white

## WHAT NOT TO DO
- No sharp corners anywhere (use rounded-2xl or rounded-3xl minimum)
- No pure white (#fff) — use warm-white (#FEFCFA) or cream (#FAF5EF)
- No blue links (use terracotta)
- No gray text (use terracotta/60 or forest/60 for muted text)
- No sans-serif headings (all headings = Fraunces)
- No perfect symmetry — embrace slight imperfection, it feels human
